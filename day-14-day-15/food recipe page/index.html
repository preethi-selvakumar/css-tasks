Tasty Pasta Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 cup pasta
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1/2 cup tomatoes (chopped)
  • 1/4 cup cheese
  • Salt & pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Boil pasta for 10 minutes and drain.
  2. Heat olive oil in a pan.
  3. Add tomatoes and cook for 2 minutes.
  4. Mix in the pasta and season with salt and pepper.
  5. Sprinkle cheese on top and serve hot.
