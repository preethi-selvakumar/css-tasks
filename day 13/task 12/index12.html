The Importance of Web Development

Web development is the process of creating and maintaining websites. It involves various technologies like HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and frameworks such as React and Angular. A well-designed website improves user experience and helps businesses grow online.

Why Learn Web Development?

In today's digital world, having a website is essential for businesses, bloggers, and professionals. Web development offers career opportunities in frontend, backend, and full-stack development. By learning web technologies, you can build interactive and user-friendly websites.
