About Sticky Headers

A sticky header remains at the top of the page while scrolling, ensuring easy navigation without taking up too much space.

Benefits of Sticky Headers

  • Improves navigation accessibility
  • Enhances user experience
  • Provides quick access to important links

How It Works

The 'position: sticky;' property makes the header stay at the top when scrolling down.
